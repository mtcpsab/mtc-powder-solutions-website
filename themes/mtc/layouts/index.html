What we do

MTC is a world-leading producer of near-net shape (NNS) products produced by hot isostatic pressing (HIP). The ability to manufacture HIP products with irregular shapes and complex geometry offers several advantages over castings, forgings and fabricated materials, both in terms of design flexibility and material properties.
